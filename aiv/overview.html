AI Castles at a Glance
Each AI character in Stronghold Crusader comes with a preinstalled set of 8 different castles. These provided castles apply to both Stronghold Crusader and Stronghold Crusader Extreme. The castle in a format known as aiv files, and these are stored in the aiv subfolder of your installation. They are named <aicharacter>X.aiv where aicharacter is the name of the character, and X is a number from 1 to 8 (ie snake1.aiv). When a match starts, the AI decides randomly (and also based on map size and terrain) which castle to build.
