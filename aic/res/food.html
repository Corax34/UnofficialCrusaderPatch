Field Value Type Range of Acceptable Values Description
MaxFood Integer 0 to 10000 Maximum amount of each different type of food the AI will keep in stock. This also includes wheat
MinimumApples Integer 0 to 11 This amount of apples are 'saved' and only consumed if current popularity < LowestPopularity. If the AI has less than this amount it will prioritize buying the missing food
MinimumCheese Integer 0 to 10000 This amount of cheese is 'saved' and only consumed if current popularity < LowestPopularity. If the AI has less than this amount it will prioritize buying the missing food
MinimumBread Integer 0 to 10000 This amount of bread is 'saved' and only consumed if current popularity < LowestPopularity. If the AI has less than this amount it will prioritize buying the missing food
MinimumWheat Integer 0 to 10000 Below this amount the AI will prioritize buying wheat until it reaches this amount
MinimumHop Integer 0 to 10000 Below this amount the AI will prioritize buying hops until it reaches this amount
TradeAmountFood Integer 0 to 10000 The amount of food, wheat or hops bought at once. Only one of these trades is made per gametick
DoubleRationsFoodThreshold Integer 0 to 231 - 1 The AI will give double rations if it has at least this much food
MaxBeer Integer 0 to 231 - 1 Max amount of beer to store on stockpile
