Field Value Type Range of Acceptable Values Description
BuildInterval Integer 0 to 231 - 1 Number of gameticks between buildsteps.
This is only considered at <=5000 gold
ResourceRebuildDelay Integer 0 to 231 - 1 The delay before the AI rebuilds destroyed resource buildings
RecruitGoldThreshold Integer 0 to 231 - 1 If the AI has less gold than this threshold it will not recruit or buy ressources for aiv buildings or economy. Sortie units, resource buildings and defensive siege engines are excluded
DefSiegeEngineGoldThreshold Integer 0 to 231 - 1 Gold for spending on AIV-defined siege engines: with positive value AI will spend its remaining gold (and cheat if necessary and gold > 0) compared to negative value which serves as a minimum gold
DefSiegeEngineBuildDelay Integer 0 to 231 - 1 The delay before the AI builds/rebuilds in the AIV placed siege engines
